About Coxwise

Helping students build plans, not just fill out forms.

Coxwise Diversified Venture Management was created by educator and founder Paul J. R. Cox Sr. to give students and families a clearer way to think about college, money, and opportunity.

After years in the classroom and in the community, Paul saw the same pattern: students with talent and drive, parents with concern and questions, and very few spaces where everyone could talk honestly about both education and income in the same conversation.

Coxwise exists to connect those dots. We use Hope AI, coaching, and planning tools to help young people build business plans and income strategies that support their education instead of fighting against it.
